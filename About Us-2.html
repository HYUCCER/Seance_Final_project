About Us

Séance (Angelo Yousef, Sean Kovacs, Parker Tozier, Clay Hollander, and Ben Sandall) initially formed to be one of those bands that doesn’t play any music, but they wear like, baseball jerseys, and do bits. But they decided to start playing emo music because emo music is very lucrative, and they plan to make hella money then get box seats at baseball games. They also enjoy Weezer music, swimming, and talking to their neighbors, Brandon, and Phil. Séance formed in 2020, and they plan to break up around 2025 or so.
